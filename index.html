About Full Stack Conference

The beautiful city of Lagos, Nigeria will be the host city for Full Stack Conf!

Explore the future of JavaScript with a lineup of industry professionals. Discover new techniques to advance your career as a web developer.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international JavaScript community and share skills with some of the world's top experts, hackers, and makers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of JavaScript.

Full Stack Conference is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

MongoDB: NoSQL database Angular: Javascript framework Express: Framework for Node Node.js: Javascript environment ES2015: Latest version of Javascript Babel: Javascript compiler

Speakers

Kate Trippier

Kate is a web developer and teacher who is passionate about building scalable, data driven web apps, especially ones that address old problems with new tech!

Cooper Zed

Cooper is a software engineer and philosopher trying to leave the world better than he found it. He codes for non-profits, eCommerce, and large-scale web apps.

Jane Underwood

Jane is a JavaScript developer working on large-scale applications. She's also a teacher who strives to support students in removing all barriers to learning code.

Peter Gray

Peter found his passion for computers and programming over 15 years ago. He is excited to introduce people to the wonderful world of JavaScript.

Michael Zenden

Michael is a developer, author of CSS: The Missing Manual, JavaScript & jQuery: The Missing Manual, and web development teacher.

Anita Clerk

All of her professional life, Anita has worked with computers online; She is a polyglot programmer and likes using the right tools for the job.
